About the Project

Personalized Nutrition Recommendation System is a groundbreaking project designed to help users achieve their health and dietary goals through customized recommendations. The platform ensures a seamless and intuitive experience with cutting-edge algorithms like KNN and collaborative filtering, helping users maintain a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
